SurveyStack Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

SurveyStack ("the App") is a field-survey data collection app published by Phoenixtek Solutions. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it's used, and who can see it.

Who operates this

SurveyStack syncs data to a server operated by the organisation that deployed it. When you create an account, you're creating it on that specific organisation's server — your data is visible only to members of your workspace, not to other organisations using the same app.

Contact: service@phoenixtek.in

Information we collect

Account information — email address, display name, and a password (stored as a salted, one-way hash, never in plain text) or, if you choose "Sign in with Google," your Google account's email and name.

Location data — GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude, accuracy) are captured when you explicitly tap a "capture GPS" control, to tag a project's site location or a photo's location. Location is never collected in the background or without you initiating the capture.

Camera and photos — photos and videos you take within the app (site photos, vehicle number-plate photos for OD surveys, etc.) are stored and synced to your workspace's server so your team can see them. Number-plate photos are processed on-device via on-device OCR to read the registration number automatically — the photo itself is also kept and synced so it can be included in generated reports.

Survey data you enter — all form fields you fill in (project details, load-test readings, traffic-survey rows, etc.) are stored and synced to your workspace's server.

Push notification token — if notifications are enabled, a device token (issued by Firebase Cloud Messaging) is registered with the server so it can notify you of project invites and access requests.

How information is used

All of the above is used solely to provide the App's core function: letting your survey team capture, sync, and share field data with each other. We do not sell data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Who can see your data

  • Other members of your workspace who have been invited to a project can see that project's data, per the access controls built into the app (project owner/member roles).
  • The workspace's administrator(s) can see all projects within that workspace.
  • Server operators (whoever runs the server your workspace is on) can access the underlying database, since data is stored there — the same as any self-hosted business tool.

Data retention and deletion

Data is retained until a project or account is deleted. To request deletion of your account or data, contact your workspace administrator, or email service@phoenixtek.in.

Third-party services

Google Sign-In (optional): if you use this login method, Google's own privacy policy also applies to that authentication step.

Firebase Cloud Messaging: used only to deliver push notifications; governed by Google's Firebase privacy terms.

Children's privacy

SurveyStack is a professional field-survey tool not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact
