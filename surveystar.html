What is SurveyStar?

SurveyStar is an Android application developed by Phoenixtek Solution for highway and infrastructure survey teams. It enables field engineers to collect road condition data directly from their mobile device — no bulky equipment required.

The app records IRI (International Roughness Index), GPS coordinates, speed, and real-time road surface condition data as you drive, making large-scale road surveys faster, more accurate, and paperless.

📍

GPS Tracking

Automatic GPS coordinate logging tied to every data point — precise location at every measurement.

📊

IRI Measurement

Records International Roughness Index (IRI) using the phone's accelerometer — no external sensors needed.

🛣️

Road Condition Data

Capture potholes, cracks, surface distress, and condition ratings in real time as you survey.

📤

Data Export

Export survey data in structured formats for reporting, GIS mapping, and project documentation.

🏗️ Built for infrastructure professionals

SurveyStar is purpose-built for the day-to-day work of highway engineers, road survey consultants, and government field teams:

  • National highway condition assessments
  • State road and rural road surveys
  • Pre and post-construction road quality checks
  • Pavement management system (PMS) data collection
  • Annual road maintenance planning surveys
