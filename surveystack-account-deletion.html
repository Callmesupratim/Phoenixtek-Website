Delete your SurveyStack account and data

SurveyStack is published by Phoenixtek Solutions.

This page explains how to request deletion of your SurveyStack account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

Send an email to service@phoenixtek.in from the email address registered to your SurveyStack account, with the subject line Delete my SurveyStack account. Please include your account email in the message body so we can locate your account.

Alternatively, if your account was created within an organisation's workspace, you may ask that workspace's administrator to remove your account.

To send a deletion request: email service@phoenixtek.in with subject Delete my SurveyStack account from your registered email address.

What gets deleted

  • Your account details: email address, display name, profile photo, and login credentials.
  • Personal survey data and media (photos/videos) that belong solely to you and are not shared with other members of your workspace.

What may be retained

  • Survey records, readings, and media that you contributed to a shared project may be retained by the organisation that owns that workspace, because other members rely on that data for their own work. To have shared project data removed, the workspace administrator (or project owner) must delete the relevant project or records.
  • Data held in routine server backups is purged in the normal backup-rotation cycle.

Timeframe

  • Account-deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.
  • Backup copies containing the deleted data are cleared within 90 days.

Contact

Questions about deletion or your data: service@phoenixtek.in
