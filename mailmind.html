What is Mailmind?

Mailmind is a Windows desktop application that connects to your email accounts and uses AI to help you read, understand, and respond to emails faster. It supports Gmail, Microsoft Outlook, Zoho Mail, Yahoo Mail, and any standard IMAP mailbox — all in one place.

Mailmind is developed by Phoenixtek Solution, a technology company based in Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

🔑 Why Mailmind requests Google account access

When you connect a Gmail account in Mailmind, the application requests permission to access your Gmail data via the official Google OAuth 2.0 flow. This access is used solely within the app on your device to:

  • Read your emails so they can be displayed inside Mailmind
  • Send emails on your behalf when you press Send
  • Manage labels and read/unread status
  • Allow AI analysis of email content (processed locally)

Mailmind does not share your Gmail data with any third party. All Google user data is used only to provide the in-app email functionality you explicitly request.

Google API permissions used

Permission (Scope) Why it is needed
gmail.readonly Read and display your emails inside the app
gmail.send Send emails on your behalf when you click Send
gmail.modify Mark emails as read/unread, apply labels

Data & Privacy

Mailmind's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not store your emails on our servers. We do not use your email content to train AI models. We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties.
