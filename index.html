About Phoenixtek Solution

Engineering trust, one measurement at a time — India's dedicated partner for geotechnical and structural safety.

Who We Are

In strategic partnership with Alfresco Construction Services Pvt. Ltd., Phoenixtek Solution stands at the forefront of geotechnical instrumentation and structural health monitoring in India. We were established with a clear purpose: to bring world-class precision monitoring technology to Indian infrastructure — making every bridge, dam, tunnel, and high-rise safer for the people who depend on them.

Our story began with a simple but powerful belief held by our founder, Supratim Mondal — that robust infrastructure safety is not a luxury but a necessity. Backed by deep domain knowledge and hands-on field experience, we have grown into a trusted name across some of India's most demanding infrastructure projects, from the plains of Bengal to the high-altitude terrains of the Northeast.

What sets us apart is our commitment to the full monitoring lifecycle: from instrument selection and installation, through real-time data acquisition, to interpretation, reporting, and actionable recommendations. We don't just install sensors — we deliver clarity.

Our Mission To deliver precise, reliable geotechnical and structural monitoring solutions that protect lives, safeguard infrastructure, and support India's sustainable development goals.
Our Vision To be India's most trusted infrastructure monitoring partner — empowering engineers and decision-makers with real-time, data-driven insights for safer, smarter construction.
