What is SurveyStack?

SurveyStack is a mobile field data collection application developed by Phoenixtek Solution for engineers, surveyors, and infrastructure project teams. It simplifies the process of recording observations, measurements, and media in the field — with GPS tagging at every step — and syncs everything to the cloud when connectivity is available.

Whether you are conducting a road inspection, bridge assessment, or infrastructure audit, SurveyStack lets your team collect structured, geo-referenced data without needing an internet connection on-site.

📍

GPS Geo-Tagging

Every data point, photo, and observation is automatically tagged with precise GPS coordinates so you always know exactly where each record was made.

🔌

Works Offline

Full functionality in the field with no internet required. All data is stored locally on the device and synced automatically when a connection is restored.

👥

Team Sync

Multiple team members can collect data simultaneously across a project. Data from all field devices consolidates in the cloud for a complete project view.

📊

Excel Export

Export your collected survey data as an Excel-compatible spreadsheet — ready for reporting, GIS import, or further analysis.

📸

Photo Documentation

Capture photos linked to each survey point, automatically stamped with location, timestamp, and project context.

📋

Structured Forms

Collect consistent, structured observations using project-specific forms — eliminating freehand notes and improving data quality.

🏗️ Built for infrastructure professionals

SurveyStack is designed for the day-to-day realities of field survey work on civil and infrastructure projects:

  • Road condition assessments and highway inspections
  • Bridge and structure visual surveys
  • Pre and post-construction site walkthroughs
  • Geotechnical field observation logging
  • Environmental baseline surveys
  • Defect mapping and snag-list documentation with GPS evidence
